About SkyScholars

SkyScholars empowers the next generation of aerospace engineers through engaging workshops, hands-on experiments, and mentorship opportunities. We've reimagined STEM education to make complex concepts accessible and exciting.

Our unique approach combines YouTube educational content with interactive demos and practical building sessions, creating a multidimensional learning experience that resonates with today's digital-native students.

Our Educational Approach

We make aerospace learning accessible and engaging through multiple innovative channels

YouTube Content

Our professionally produced videos break down complex aerospace concepts into digestible, entertaining content that students love to watch and share.

Interactive Demos

Web-based simulations and interactive models allow students to experiment with aerodynamics principles in a risk-free digital environment.

Hands-On Building

We provide kits and materials for students to apply their knowledge by designing and building physical models, reinforcing theoretical concepts.

Our Programs

Aerodynamics Basics

Interactive lessons and demos explaining lift, drag, thrust, and flight control principles through engaging digital content and physical experiments.

  • Virtual wind tunnel simulations
  • Paper airplane design challenges
  • Video series on flight principles

Model Building

Workshops where students design and build model planes, learning engineering fundamentals through practical application.

  • 3D design software tutorials
  • Build-along video series
  • Virtual testing before building

Career Exploration

Meet real engineers and pilots, explore career paths, and ask questions during live sessions and virtual field trips.

  • Industry professional Q&A sessions
  • Virtual tours of aerospace facilities
  • Career pathway guidance
